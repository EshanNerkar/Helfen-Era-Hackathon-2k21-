A Brief Insight


Here , in this world where every single person is running towards digitalization, the gap between the rich and the poor is increasing exponentially. The main cause of this is that these poor people do not even have a basic source of income , which is why there are in no position to compete with the richer class.


This Website

Is created with an aim to decrease this gap between the poor class and the rich class, and in order to do that we have to make sure that these poor people have at least the most basic means of employment so that it will not only help these poor people to cope with the basic financial problems but also keep a positive morals among them. So here, we will act as an intermediate between the people who have these basic needs such as shelter, food and employment that need to be fulfilled and those people who can fulfill those needs.
