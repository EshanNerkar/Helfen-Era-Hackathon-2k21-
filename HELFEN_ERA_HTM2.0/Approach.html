Our Approach

Poverty has been an issue faced by India for quite some time now. This is mainly due to illiteracy,the exponentially increasing population and also the unemployment.Due to which some people are deprived of their basic needs and this causes more illiteracy and also causes the gap between the rich and poor to increase .

According to us, these basic needs such as food, shelter and education are every individual’s rights,these rights make them empowered in the long term.So, we have decided to take up this social cause and have made some efforts to decrease the poverty index. Our aim is that every person can get these basic facilities,and get themselves a better standard of living.

Our website,Helfen Era has taken up this challenge to associate people with organizations which will provide every people in need a shelter ,food,education and a better way of living..Here, we welcome all the sponsors and volunteers to lend their helping hand in this social cause...So, we can make India economically and socially strong again . As we know, malnutrition is also an issue caused by lack of proper nutrition and food. Whereas 5-star restaurants waste a large amount of food on a daily basis.So,we have decided that this extra food could be made available to the needy by our volunteers where we shall act as a medium between these organizations. We have some governmental and non governmental organisations supporting this campaign .

We will be acting as an intermediary between the organizations and poor people so that no individual gets deprived of these basic amenities. The sponsors will be providing funds, and also, on their level, they shall help these people , and make sure that all of the amount they are pitching in is utilized in providing food,shelter and education to the poor.

Children of today are the adults of tomorrow. It is these children who will shape the future of India. It is therefore imperative that every last child in India gets a healthy and fair start in life. Educating children will go a long way in empowering them to grow into skilled, mature and capable adults who can contribute better to their own and society’s welfare.Hence, we will try connecting these children with these organizations and provide them with the best education possible,so that they get a better tomorrow . This is because money can’t eradicate poverty but education definitely can.This way we are trying to get rid of poverty,by,making everyone self-sufficient so they can get a better life and adapt a better standard of living.We accept all the people who want to voluntarily participate and donate in this social concerns so we emerge as a stronger nation .
